Pages:
1. Employee Management Page: This page will allow HR to manage employee records, including adding new employees, updating existing records, and viewing employee details.
2. Leave Application Page: This page will allow employees to submit their leave applications. They will fill out a form with their personal details, leave type, start date, and duration. Upon submission, the application will be sent for approval.
3. Awaiting Approval Page: This page will display all leave applications that are pending approval.
4. Approved Applications Page: This page will display all leave applications that have been approved, along with their details and approval status.
5. Leave Application Details Page: This page will show the details of a specific leave application,
    including the employee's information, leave type, start date, duration, and approval status. It will also provide options for HR to approve or reject the application.
6. Leave Application History Page: This page will allow employees to view their past leave applications, including the status of each application (approved, rejected, or pending). It will also provide a summary of total leave taken and remaining leave balance.
